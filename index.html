Welcome to Rajec

Rajec is a beautiful town located in Slovakia. It is known for its rich history, stunning natural landscapes, and warm hospitality.

About Rajec

Rajec is situated in the Žilina Region of Slovakia. It is surrounded by picturesque mountains, making it a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts.

Things to Do

  • Explore the historic Rajec Castle
  • Take a hike in the nearby Malá Fatra National Park
  • Visit the Rajec Spa for relaxation and wellness
  • Experience the local culture and traditions at the Rajec Folk Festival

Getting Here

Rajec is easily accessible by road and public transportation. The nearest airport is Žilina Airport, which is approximately 30 kilometers away.

Accommodation

There are several hotels, guesthouses, and holiday rentals available in Rajec to suit every budget and preference.
